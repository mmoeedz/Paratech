FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things prospects ask us most often.

How fast can you start?

Most engagements kick off within 5–10 business days of a signed agreement. Rush options are available.

What does it cost?

Projects start around $5K; ongoing programs from $1.5K/mo. We'll quote precisely after the discovery call.

Are there contracts?

Project work is fixed-scope. Retainers are month-to-month after a 90-day initial term. No long lock-ins.

Do you work outside the US?

Yes. Most clients are US-based, but we work globally — primarily in English-speaking markets.

Who will I work with?

You'll have a dedicated account manager and direct access to the specialists doing the actual work.

How is success measured?

We agree on KPIs upfront — revenue, leads, rankings, ROAS — and report against them transparently.
